<p>The Video Block allows you to put videos from different sources on your website.</p>
<p>How to add a video
After dragging The Video Block on the page, you can open its settings by clicking on the block. Insert the link to the video you want on your page into the text field. Alternatively, you can use the embed code of the video. Supported video platforms are YouTube, Vimeo and Dailymotion.
Video appearance
It is possible to stretch the video across the width of the website. You can also change the aspect ratio of the video.
Advanced settings
In the advanced settings, the ID can be very useful to you. It is used to create a link using an anchor. You can learn more about setting up and using anchors in this article.</p>
